
CLASSPATH SETTINGS

To build the files in this book, you must add the Jar file
	com-darwinsys-util.jar
into your classpath. Please update your classpath settings
(referring to Recipe 2.x if necessary), and try again.

You can get the jar file from the book website, 
http://www.darwinsys.com/javacook, or by building it
in the subdirectory "com.darwinsys.util" here.
